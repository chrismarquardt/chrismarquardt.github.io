Let us help you supercharge your remote video productions

OBS Ninja is a free system that allows you to add remote guests to your video productions

* at super low latency (often under 200ms)
* with excellent video quality (up to 4k30)
* with studio-quality audio
* using existing cameras and microphones

No plugin or software installation required, no login needed. OBS Ninja works in Chrome browser, right out of the box, integrating into OBS and other video production suites. You can even use it to turn your phone into a remote camera. For free.

What makes OBS Ninja so special?
It's all in the quality, control and flexibility!

OBS Ninja can be used to...

  • ... set up a simple two-way video conference in seconds
    This session can have amazing audio/video quality with very low latency, so you can have fluid remote conversations that don't suffer from long delays. It also doesn't need any installations, logins, accounts or passwords.
  • ... bring remote video and audio into OBS
    You can bring remote video right into OBS to then do video switching, complex scene compositing, recording and even live streaming to online services like YouTube or Twitch. OBS can also output to a virtual webcam that can again be streamed over OBS Ninja. The possibilities are almost endless.
  • ... create a virtual green room for your guests
    Set up a virtual green room for your show's guests and transfer them in and out of the live show with a click of a button.
  • ... safely communicate
    Communication between peers is point-to-point and encrypted, so not only will you get the fastest path for your streams, but also the safest.
  • ... create an ad-hoc group video or audio chat
    You and your friends can meet online in your very own group chat room, without the need for an account, a login or any special software to install. You can even share your screens. 
  • ... create a virtual teaching environment in which you have full control 
    You can use OBS Ninja to teach groups of students and share your screen. Add individual students to your screen for presentations or discussions.
  • ... add your Android or iPhone as a remote camera
    OBS Ninja runs on smartphones, so they can be used as remote sources for video and audio
  • ... build complex setups with iframes
    If you're inclined to do so, you can set up your very own solution using some javascript and iframes. OBS Ninja provides for communication between the web page and the embedded OBS Ninja videos.
  • ... create a remote camera based on a Raspberry Pi
    Yes, even that is possible. Hook up your webcam to a RasPi, install the according software and create an automated remote camera that will send high-quality video to you over the internet.

The basics

What you need to get started with OBS Ninja in general
A Computer

OBS Ninja works in Google Chrome browser on Windows and on the Mac. Depending on your system (CPU/GPU), the video quality will vary. We will help you figure out what your system can do and if it's sufficient for your planned project. The beauty of OBS Ninja is that it's extremely flexible. It can often be adapted to the available resources.

An Internet Connection

Your internet connection (both speed and stability) will determine the video and audio quality that can be transferred as well as the number of guests that you can host. We recommend a cable-bound LAN connection with at least 10/50mbps speed.

A Camera

OBS Ninja will be happy with webcams as well as with more professional mirrorless or DSLR cameras that can feed their output into your computer as a webcam. Most modern bigger cameras do that, either by installing additional software or through an HDMI2USB converter.

Optional (but helpful)
A Microphone

A dedicated microphone will always sound better than the one that's built into your computer or your camera. With years in professional audio and video production, we are happy to make recommendations that don't break the bank.

Headphones

A common audio issue in conferences are unwanted echoes and feedback. A pair of closed-back headphones or earphones will solve this issue. If that is not an option, we will be able to help mitigate echoes by employing OBS Ninja's built-in echo cancellation.

Light

You can massively improve the video quality of your guests by carefully watching the light setup. This can be as simple as asking a guest to turn their setup towards a window and can be as extensive as adding LED light panels.
