Supercharge your remote audio & video productions

OBS Ninja is a free system that enables you to add remote guests to your audio/video productions

* super low latency
* excellent video quality
* studio-quality audio
* use existing cameras and microphones

No plugin or software installation required, no login needed. It works right out of the box, integrating into OBS and other audio and video production suites. You can even use it to turn your phone into a remote camera. For free.

Why OBS Ninja?

Simply said: OBS Ninja is a remote audio and video swiss army knife and nothing else can match it.
It's a truly enabling technology.
And it's free. (We can thank Steve Seguin for that)

OBS Ninja enables remote productions without expensive hardware and at a quality level that goes far beyond what today's remote conferencing systems like Zoom or Skype can offer.

And all you need is a web browser.

It lets you include remote guests at stunningly low latencies (no more three seconds wait time and stumbling over each other's words)

It lets you receive remote guest video at 1080p quality.

It lets you receive remote screen shares at 1080p quality and beyond.

It lets you choose the quality level for video and audio within the capabilities of the infrastructure.

Receive 20 mbit/s video or 256 kbit/s audio? As long as your infrastructure supports it, OBS Ninja does too.

OBS Ninja in Action

One remote video participant

Remote guest (left) at 1080p30, video bitrate 3000kbps, audio at 128kbps, minimal latency. Switching/compositing live in OBS, simultaneous 1080p live stream to YouTube. OBS program streams back to the guest using a separate OBS Ninja channel.

Four international participants

Remote guests for this production are located on several continents (UK, Ireland, USA). Video bitrates are kept at an extreme minimum (~300 kbps each) to save bandwidth. Audio is at 32 kbps per guest. Switching, compositing and recording is done in OBS.

The person behind OBS Ninja

OBS Ninja is the brainchild of Steve Seguin in Toronto, Canada. He is an expert in computer vision and has spent considerable time developing deep learning solutions. Steve's current position is Innovation Officer at Stage TEN. OBS Ninja is his passion project. With the Covid-19 pandemic and the renewed interest in remote high-quality video, development on OBS Ninja has received a massive boost.

Steve provides OBS Ninja for free on GitHub where you can find more resources and support the project.

Steve is not directly affiliated with this consulting service, but we keep in close contact.
